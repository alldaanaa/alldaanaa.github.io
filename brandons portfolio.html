Hi, I'm Brandon

I've built three brands from scratch—LaPatronaCMX, Esfuerzo, and South Bay Run Club. As Social Media Content Lead and Branding/Marketing Lead for all three, I create social media content that drives real community engagement and builds authentic connections.

My social media content has generated 2.2M+ views on individual campaigns, brought 350+ people together for in-person events, and earned features on Food Network, NBC, and Telemundo.

I don't just create content—I build movements. Life responds to effort.

Selected Work

Building communities through content creation, storytelling and action

Cinco de Mile

Created Gilroy's inaugural community race from concept to execution. Secured city council sponsorship and Mayor attendance. The event brought together 300+ runners and has become an annual city tradition.

Event Production · Community Building · 300+ Participants

LaPatronaCMX Viral Campaign

Developed and executed viral content strategy for LaPatronaCMX food truck. The campaign generated 2.2M+ views and established the brand as a Bay Area culinary destination. Earned features on Food Network and national media.

Content Strategy · Viral Marketing · 2.2M+ Views

South Bay Run Club

Built and scaled the premier weekly running community in the Bay Area. Developed brand partnerships with Voyager Craft Coffee and Skratch Labs. Consistently brings together 80-150+ runners every Saturday with 179K+ monthly reach.

Community Building · Brand Partnerships · 179K+ Monthly Reach

Esfuerzo 5K

Produced pilot race in partnership with nonprofit Viva Calle SJ. The success of the event (250+ participants) secured an invitation to become part of their annual events starting 2026.

Event Production · Nonprofit Partnership · 250+ Participants
